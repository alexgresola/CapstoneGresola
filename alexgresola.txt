To start:
1.	Open www.gethub.com
 


2.	Type a username, email and password to create account
 







3.	You will receive an email that will verify the account
 
4.	Click verify
 
To become a collaborator:
1.	Find the account that you want to add
 



2.	Click Follow
 

3.	To see if you already followed that account the Follow button will change to Unfollow
 


4.	To be a watcher in that Account click the Watch button
